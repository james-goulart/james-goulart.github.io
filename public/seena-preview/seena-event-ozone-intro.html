Introduction to Ozone Therapy — What It Is and Who It's For

Thursday, 14 May 2026 18:30 – 20:00 Seena Centre, Parque das Nações

Join us for an accessible, jargon-free introduction to ozone therapy — one of the most versatile and evidence-backed integrative treatments available today. This session is designed for anyone curious about ozone therapy who wants honest, medical-grade information before deciding if it's right for them.

What you'll learn

  • What medical ozone therapy actually is — and what it isn't
  • How ozone works in the body: oxygen delivery, inflammation, and immune modulation
  • The different administration methods (IV, rectal, intramuscular, local) and when each is used
  • Which conditions respond best to ozone therapy
  • Safety, contraindications, and what to expect from a session at Seena
  • How ozone therapy fits into a broader integrative care plan

Who is this for?

This event is for anyone who has heard about ozone therapy and wants to understand it properly before booking — or anyone with a chronic condition looking to understand their options. No prior knowledge required. The session will be in English with Q&A.

Format

90-minute session with a 20-minute presentation, a clinical demonstration, and an extended Q&A. You're encouraged to bring your medical history or specific questions. Light refreshments provided.
